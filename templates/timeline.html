Timeline for your stay in hospital

This is an example of what happens during your hospital visit.

  • Day before admission

    Sunday 2 September 2018

    Before coming to hospital • bring all of your medication with you • stock up the freezer with precooked food or easy to cook meals for when you return • pack your mobile phone and charger • pack plenty of clothes and toiletries • if you have pets, arrange for someone to look them while you are in hospital

  • Admission day

    Monday 3 September 2018

    People you will see on your first day • a nurse who will conduct an assessment and give you a care plan • a doctor who will assess your condition and start your treatment • a consultant who will check that everything is going as planned

  • Receiving treatment

    Tuesday 4 September 2018 to Thursday 6 September 2018

    During treatment • you will be continuously monitored by the nurse • your care plan will be followed • the doctor will assess your progress • you may also be seen by other specialists, such as physiotherapists, to ensure you're making good progress

  • Getting ready to leave

    Thursday 6 September 2018

    Before coming to hospital • make sure your family or friends are aware you're leaving • arrange transport from hospital • ask someone to ensure you have basic supplies if you're going home • let the nurse know if you have any problems or queries

  • Leaving hospital

    Friday 7 September 2017

    Before you leave • get a written discharge instructions and a summary of your current health status • check if you have any follow up appointments • make sure you understand what medication you need to take and when • if you're going home ask if you will get any care such as community nursing or GP visits • ask about things to watch for and what to do about them • write down the number of the person to call if you have any problems
