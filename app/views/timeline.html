Timeline for your stay in hospital

This is an example of what happens during your hospital visit.

Day before admission

Sunday 2nd September
Before coming to hospital
  • bring all of you medication with you
  • stock up the freezer with precooked food or easy to cook meals for when you return
  • pack your mobile phone and charger
  • pack plenty of clothes and toiletries
  • arrange for someone to look after your pets while you are in hospital

Admission day

Monday 3rd September
People you will be seen by on your first day
  • a nurse who will conduct an assessment and give you a care plan
  • a doctor who will assess your condition and start your treatment
  • a consultant who will check that everything is going as planned

Treatment days

Tuesday 4th September to Saturday 8th September
During treatment
  • you will be continuously monitored by the nurse
  • your care plan will be followed
  • the doctor will assess your progress
  • you may also be seen by other specialists, such as physiotherapists, to ensure you're making good progress

Getting ready to leave

Sunday 9th September
Getting ready to leave hospital
  • make sure your family or friends are aware you're leaving
  • arrange transport from hospital
  • ask someone to ensure you have basic supplies if you're going home
  • let the nurse know if you have any problems or queries

Leaving hospital

Monday 10th September
Leaving hospital - hooray!
  • get a written discharge instructions and a summary of your current health status
  • check if you have any follow up appointments
  • make sure you understand what medication you need to take and when
  • if you're going home ask if you will get any care such as community nursing or GP visits
  • ask about things to watch for and what to do about them
  • write down the number of the person to call if you have any problems
  • buy nice chocolate for the nurses!
